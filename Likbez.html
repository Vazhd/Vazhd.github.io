About us


The LikBez language School network was founded in 2012. We provide professional English language courses for all levels. LikBez teachers are native English speakers from the UK, USA, Canada, Australia and New Zealand. They have international qualifications in the field of teaching, as well as experience in teaching children and adults in different countries of the world. The use of communicative methods in the study of foreign languages is the main focus of LikBez.
